About GrapeHealth

GrapeHealth is a mobile application (Android ) which is designed to serve farmers, agricultural engineers, government’s agricultural consultants, agricultural groups, grape merchants and drug companies or chemical stores. The innovative part of GrapeHealth is integrating data, image and agronomy to diagnose grape diseases. The main purpose of the application is recognizing the disease of the grape plants and informing the users about their grape condition by either making them to take photos of the leaf or using our diagnosis feature.

WHY IS IT IMPORTANT ?

GrapeHealth aims to improve the grape producers experience and knowledge with grape related diseases.

Comprehensive features will help the users to keep an eye on their farms.

Detecting diseases will improve efficiency and quality by adding value to fresh and healthy grapes.

WHAT IS GRAPEHEALTH ?

GrapeHealth is a mobile application for people who are or interested in grape production.

Users are able to detect their grape’s disease by uploading a leaf image or answering diagnosis questionnaire.

Users can add their farms and learn the weather details or nearby diseases to be aware of any threat.

Core Features

Upload a photo of the grape leaf and identify its disease
By answering the diagnosis questionnarre, detect local diseases

Add the vineyard location to see the diseases occuring nearby
Be notified about the real-time local weather conditions

Learn treatment options and disease details
Monitor vineyards and weather conditions to prevent diseases
